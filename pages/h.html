The <H> Tag

  • Hierarchy of tags
  • SEO and Accessibility
  • Default Styling

Heading tags are used to define headings and subheadings on a web page, helping to structure the content and indicate its hierarchy. They play a vital role in both SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and accessibility. By default, browsers apply various font sizes and styles to these tags, but you can customize them using CSS.
